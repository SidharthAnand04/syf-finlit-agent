## Credit Card Types

### Synchrony Mastercards
General purpose credit cards accepted anywhere Mastercard is accepted. Suitable for everyday purchases and earning rewards across all spending categories.

### Synchrony Network Cards (Retail / Store Cards)
Store-specific credit cards that can only be used at the partnering retailer. Often feature special financing promotions such as deferred interest or no-interest-if-paid-in-full (NIPIF) promotional periods. Examples include cards for major retailers across home improvement, electronics, furniture, jewelry, and more.

### CareCredit®
A healthcare credit card designed for medical, dental, vision, hearing, veterinary, and other health-related expenses. Offers promotional financing options (typically 6, 12, 18, or 24 months deferred interest) for qualifying purchases at enrolled healthcare providers.

### Synchrony Project Card
Designed for home improvement projects. Helps finance larger renovations or repairs with flexible payment options at participating home improvement retailers and contractors.

### Synchrony HOME™
A credit card for home furnishings, flooring, appliances, and home décor purchases at participating retailers. Often includes special financing offers on larger purchases.

### Synchrony Car Care™

### Synchrony Luxury

### Synchrony Outdoors®

## Other Financing Options

### Synchrony Pay Later (Buy Now, Pay Later)
Installment loan option allowing shoppers to split purchases into fixed monthly payments. Available at participating online and in-store retailers.

### Deferred Interest Promotions
Many Synchrony retail cards offer "no interest if paid in full" promotional periods (e.g., 6, 12, 18, 24 months). Important: if the balance is not paid in full by the end of the promotional period, interest is charged back to the original purchase date. This differs from true 0% APR offers.
